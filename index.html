How Recommendations Are Calculated

Each pair receives a composite score (0–1) based on:
• 40% Drought— How many draws since it last appeared (longer = higher)
• 30% Frequency Deviation— Underrepresented vs expected (more under = higher)
• 15% Cold Momentum— If its digits are currently cold in last 100 draws
• 15% Trend Reversion— If its digits are trending below expected (mean-reversion signal)
